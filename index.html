PICTOREAL WEB TEAM

It is with immense pleasure that we present to you volume-21 of the annual magazine, PICTOREAL. Team Pictoreal has been working untiringly throughout the year to make each and every event a great success. Though the events like blood donation, Pict-o-social (interaction with school children), money donation are mainly organized to extend a helping hand to a certain section of society; but, we believe; that it is more important that the events invoke a thought of social responsibility within our volunteers which they carry throughout their lives. Pics-O-Reel has been providing a platform to students to showcase their skills and creativity for the past few years; however; this year, we got an opportunity to present the selected photographs/sketches to all the stake holders during the “Open Day” celebration at PICT. On a personal note, this was a beginning for me and I hope to contribute to the best of my ability to bring out the best in students and faculty through PICTOREAL.

BE PHOTOSHOOT BE Photoshoot is a farewell to the BEs. PICTOREAL organizes a photoshoot for the graduating batch, the photos of which are printed in the magazine. Spread over few hours, the event witnesses BEs dressed as their best. The event is thoroughly enjoyed by the BEs who are busy clicking pictures and making memories of a lifetime. The magazine then acts as a memento to their life spent at PICT.
