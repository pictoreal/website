INTRODUCTION

A magazine- a relevant one-should be a sound in itself and not just an echo. PICTOREAL is a community of artists, writers, social workers and creative thinkers who voice the activities in and around PICT.

There’s a lot more to college than its curriculum. At PICTOREAL, we take up the responsibility of creating a collage by putting together articles, interviews, write-ups, pictures and document the various phases that form an integral part of a student’s life in PICT. Therefore, the best way to discover our college is through our annual magazine. Our club promotes the students and faculty members to showcase and hone their creative and artistic skills by acknowledging their work in our magazine.

We conduct editing, designing, career guidance workshops, donation drives, photoshoots, exhibitions and competitions throughout the year with an aim to promote a constructive, healthy and competitive atmosphere in the college. Our members in PICTOREAL always live by one motto-‘ May Thoughts, Colours and Words prevail!’

PICTOSOCIAL

“Not all of us can do great things, but we can do small things with great love.”

Pictosocial is the part of Pictoreal that conducts various activities to instill the importance of social service among students. Pictosocial is a group of self-motivated individuals who willingly come forward to offer help to various sections of the society.

All the Pictosocial activities conducted throughout the year are a result of hard work of the volunteers and members. Their willingness to serve the society is what propels us to work more.

“Ideas are born and nurtured but only through a Manthan within.”

Manthan, Pictoreal’s prestigious annual public speaking event, encourages PICTians to speak in order to test their imaginative as well as public speaking skills. The event was conducted on January 16, 2020.

“A picture is a poem without words”

Pics-O-Reel, an annual art and photography exhibition organized by team Pictoreal was conducted on 20th January , it received around 340 entries and a huge response of about 800 visitors.

The Principal appreciated our team for the organization and management of the event. For the first time a QRbased voting platform was developed by SE students which replaced the manual voting process.

"Blood Donation will cost you nothing, but it will save a life!”

The annual blood Donation Camp was organized on February 10, 2020 by Pictoreal in collaboration with NSS and with the help of Poona Serological Blood Bank at PICT.

The event witnessed 364 bottles of blood being donated on a single day. The success of the event was so massive that it was showcased on the college website for the first time.

The annual Donation Drive of Pictoreal spanning from February 03, 2020 to February 29, 2020. The drive aimed at collecting all sorts of benefactions including money, clothes, etc from those who wished to contribute to providing for the less fortunate residents of the city.
