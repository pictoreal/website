    About Us

    A magazine is an experiment and represents a new focus, a new ratio between commerce and intellect.

    Pictoreal is one of the non-technical clubs in PICT. At pictoreal, we create and publish an annual magazine of PICT with unique themes every year. Pictoreal organizes several enthusiastic events throughout the year to boost creative minds. The club always focuses on promoting one’s skills in literature, designing, and photography. The club always supports individuals to build practical skills like event management, public oration, teamwork, and much more. To enhance the productive skills, we organize multiple events like Pics-o-Reel, an annual art and photography exhibition where students can showcase their art pieces, Manthan, an event to promote public speaking skills which consist of mini-events like extempore, debates, group discussions, etc. Pictosocial, a subgroup of Pictoreal believes the culture in PICT to lose ourselves in the service of others till we discover our own lives and our happiness. To promote the same, we organize events like Tree plantation drives, blood donation and Money donation drives, and Old-age or Orphanage visits. To guide and communicate with students in remote areas, we organize Career counselling sessions. Here at Pictoreal, we try to step forward, reach out and help the students in PICT unleash their creative side.

    Our members in PICTOREAL always live by one motto-

    ‘May Thoughts, Colours and Words prevail!’

    Our Events

    • Career Mantra

      Career Mantra

      Career Mantra was conducted on 11th October 2019. The 2 hours long session focused on ‘Development and Competitive Coding’. Around 130 students and the speakers were present for te event. The speakers were 6 final year students who were divided into groups of 3 to help students understand the various domains and learn from their seniors’ experiences.

    • Manthan

      Manthan

      “Ideas are born and nurtured but only through a Manthan within.” Manthan, Pictoreal’s prestigious annual public speaking event, encourages PICTians to speak in order to test their imaginative as well as public speaking skills. The event was conducted on January 16, 2020.

    • Pics-O-Reel

      Pics-O-Reel

      “A picture is a poem without words” Pics-O-Reel, an annual art and photography exhibition organized by team Pictoreal was conducted on 20th January , it received around 340 entries and a huge response of about 800 visitors. The Principal appreciated our team for the organization and management of the event. For the first time a QRbased voting platform was developed by SE students which replaced the manual voting process.

    • Blood Donation

      Blood Donation

      "Blood Donation will cost you nothing, but it will save a life!” The annual blood Donation Camp was organized on February 10, 2020 by Pictoreal in collaboration with NSS and with the help of Poona Serological Blood Bank at PICT. The event witnessed 364 bottles of blood being donated on a single day. The success of the event was so massive that it was showcased on the college website for the first time.

    • Donation Drive

      Donation Drive

      "Happiness does not come from what we get, but from what we give.” The annual Donation Drive of Pictoreal spanning from February 03, 2020 to February 29, 2020. The drive aimed at collecting all sorts of benefactions including money, clothes, etc from those who wished to contribute to providing for the less fortunate residents of the city.

    • BE Photoshoot

      BE Photoshoot

      The most awaited BE Photoshoot took place on February 25, 2020. Team Pictoreal and eight hundred BE students were on the campus, ready for the photoshoot with the esteemed presence of the faculty and other staff.
